# Privacy Policy

## Your Privacy Matters

Our mission is to bridge the gap between education and execution by connecting builders, students, founders, and professionals. We are committed to being transparent about the data we collect about you, how it is used, and with whom it is shared.

## 1. Data We Collect

- Email address

- OTP (6-digit code for email verification during signup)

**Onboarding**
When you complete onboarding, you provide:
- **Name:** First name, last name

- **Role Selection:** One or more of Student, Founder, Working Professional, Freelancer

**Role-Specific Details:**

*Student:*
- Education level (college/high school)

- Field of study

- Interests/topics

*Founder:*

- Industry
- Stage (idea, pre-seed, seed, Series A+)

*Working Professional:*
- Job title
- Company name
- Industry
- Location
- Employment type (full-time, contract, freelance, internship)
- Experience level
- Work mode (on-site, hybrid, remote)
- Career goals

*Freelancer:*
- Primary skill
- Category
- Freelance type
- Hourly/project rate
- Portfolio URL
- Location
- Availability status
- Project preferences

**Profile Information**
- Headline/bio (max 100 characters)

- Experience (job title, company, dates, location, work mode)

- Location

**Content You Create**
- Posts (text, images, videos, short videos, articles, events, products, work)

**Messages**
- Text messages

**Job Applications**
- Resume/CV (PDF, DOC, DOCX - max 10MB)
- Cover letter
- Application details

## 2. How We Use Your Data

### 2.1 Providing Our Services

**Account Management**
- Create and maintain your account
- Authenticate your login
- Manage password reset
- Handle account lockout after failed login attempts (4 failed attempts = 30 minute lockout)

**Onboarding**
- Guide you through profile setup
- Save your progress if you exit midway
- Resume from last incomplete step on next login

**Personalization**
- Recommend connections based on your profile and interests
- Personalize your feed with relevant content
- Suggest jobs matching your skills and preferences
- Recommend communities and events

**Notifications**
We send you notifications for:
- Likes, comments, replies on your posts
- Trending posts
- New followers and connection requests
- Community joins and new posts in communities
- Mentions in posts/comments
- Messages
- Event RSVPs, reminders (30 min, 24 hours before), updates
- Job alerts matching your preferences
- Application status updates
- New device login alerts
- Product comments, launch upvotes

You can control notification preferences in Settings.

**Notification Actions:**
- Mark as Read
- Turn Off This Type (mute notification category)
- Mute user (stop notifications from specific user)
- Block user
- Delete notification
- Report user/content
- Filter by category: All, Activities, Connections, Events, Mentions
- Filter by date: Today, Yesterday, Last 7 Days, Last 30 Days, This Month, Last Month, Custom

**Messaging**
- Enable direct messages, group chats, and community chats
- Show online status and typing indicators
- Process read receipts
- Show last seen time

### 2.2 Core Platform Features

**Feed and Posts**
- Display posts in your feed
- Support post types: text, media, short_video, article, event, product, work
- Process reactions: like (👍), celebrate (🎉), support (💪), love (❤️), insightful (💡), funny (😂)
- Manage post visibility: everyone, connections only

**Profile**
- Display your profile with tabs: Activities, About, Work, Posts
- Show badges (verification, crown for pro users, medal for campus reps)
- Display connection count and list
- Connection states: not connected, pending, connected, blocked
- Edit experience, education, skills, social links

**Communities**
- Create and join communities
- Community roles: member, moderator, admin
- Manage posting access
- Community visibility: public, private

**Events**
- Create events with RSVP
- Event states: draft, published, cancelled, completed
- RSVP states: going, interested, not going

**Work/Jobs**
- Post job listings (full-time, contract, freelance, internship, co-founder)
- Company profiles with size, stage, verification
- Application statuses: Applied, Under Review, Shortlisted, Interview, Offered, Rejected
- Job preferences: role, location, salary range, skills, experience, company size (1-10 to 5000+), investment stage (seed to IPO), remote culture toggle, responsiveness toggle
- Job cards show: company logo, tags, location, work mode, salary range, hiring signals
- Resume upload (PDF, DOC, DOCX - max 10MB)
- Visa sponsorship status

- Analyze usage patterns

- Conduct research and analytics

### 2.4 Security and Compliance

- Detect and prevent fraud and abuse
- Enforce our User Agreement
