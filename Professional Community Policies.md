# The developer Community Guidelines

**Effective Date:** April 13, 2026

## 1. Purpose

Our platform is a community of builders, students, founders, and professionals. We expect all users to:
- Treat each other with respect
- Provide honest feedback
- Help each other succeed
- Maintain professional conduct

---

## 2. Content Standards

### 2.1 What We Allow

- Genuine Proof of Work (projects, portfolios, case studies)
- Professional discussions about building, startups, careers
- Helpful feedback and constructive criticism
- Authentic connections and networking
- Job opportunities and freelance work

### 2.2 What We Don't Allow

**Harassment**
- Personal attacks, bullying, or intimidation
- Doxing (sharing someone's private information)
- Hate speech based on race, gender, religion, etc.

**Harmful Content**
- Violence or threats
- Dangerous or illegal goods/services
- Malware or viruses

**Deception**
- Fake profiles or impersonation
- Plagiarized work
- False information or misinformation
- Fake job postings

**Spam**
- Mass unsolicited messages
- Repeated irrelevant posts
- Artificial engagement (bot farms, like buying)

**Inappropriate Content**
- Nudity or sexual content
- Graphic violence
- Shocking or gory content

---

## 3. Roles and Responsibilities

### 3.1 Regular Users

- Follow these guidelines
- Report violations
- Engage authentically

### 3.2 Community Admins

Community admins can:
- Remove posts that violate guidelines
- Remove problematic members
- Update community settings
- Approve/deny membership requests

Admins cannot:
- Access private user data
- Violate these guidelines
- Create fake engagement

### 3.3 Event Organizers

Event hosts can:
- Set event details and requirements
- Manage RSVPs
- Remove attendees who violate guidelines

---

## 4. Enforcement

### 4.1 Consequences

If you violate these guidelines, we may:
- Remove the content
- Warn you
- Temporarily restrict your account
- Permanently ban your account

### 4.2 How We Detect Violations

- User reports
- Automated systems
- Manual review

### 4.3 Reporting

If you see something that violates these guidelines:
- Use the "Report" button on the content
- Or contact us at hatch4821@gmail.com

---

## 5. Specific Rules

### 5.1 Profiles

- Use your real name
- Upload genuine photos of yourself
- Only claim work you actually did
- Provide accurate professional information

### 5.2 Work/Portfolios

- Only upload your own work
- Give credit for collaborative work
- Don't exaggerate your contributions
- Be transparent about AI-assisted work

### 5.3 Communities

- Stay on topic
- Don't spam
- Follow community-specific rules
- Be respectful in discussions

### 5.4 Events

- Provide accurate event information
- Don't host fake events
- Follow capacity limits
- Be responsive to attendees

### 5.5 Jobs

- Post genuine job opportunities
- Provide accurate company information
- Don't disguise unpaid work as paid
- Be responsive to applicants

### 5.6 Messages

- Don't send spam
- Respect others' privacy
- Don't send unwanted advances
- Keep conversations professional

---

## 6. Appeals

If your account is restricted, you can contact us to appeal. We review appeals on a case-by-case basis.
