# The developer Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** April 13, 2026

## Your Privacy Matters

Our mission is to bridge the gap between education and execution by connecting builders, students, founders, and professionals. We are committed to being transparent about the data we collect about you, how it is used, and with whom it is shared.

This Privacy Policy applies when you use our Services. We offer you choices about the data we collect, use, and share.

---

## 1. Data We Collect

### 1.1 Data You Provide to Us

**Account Registration**
When you create an account, you provide your email address and create a password. You may also register using Google or Apple login, in which case we receive your profile information (name, email, avatar) from those providers. If you use Apple's "Hide My Email" feature, we collect the relay email address generated by Apple.

**Profile Information**
Based on your selected role(s), you provide:
- **Student:** School/college name, degree, field of study, graduation year, education level (college/high school), interests/topics
- **Founder:** Startup name, description, industry, stage, team size, funding stage, website
- **Working Professional:** Job title, company name, industry, location, experience level, employment type, career goals
- **Freelancer:** Primary skill, category, freelance type, hourly/project rate, portfolio URL, location, availability status, project preferences

**Onboarding Data**
During onboarding, you may provide additional details like profile picture, bio/headline, interests, and join communities.

**Content You Create**
- Posts (text, images, media)
- Blog articles
- Work/portfolio items (projects, case studies)
- Comments and reactions
- Event listings and RSVPs
- Job applications

**Messages**
We collect text, images, audio (up to 10 minutes), documents, and location data you share in direct messages, group chats, and community chats.

### 1.2 Data We Collect Automatically

**Usage Data**
When you use our app, we collect:
- Device information (device type, OS version)
- IP address
- Login times and session duration
- Interactions (posts you view, profiles you visit, communities you join, searches you perform)

**Cookies and Tracking**
We use cookies and similar technologies to recognize you and remember your preferences. You can opt out through browser/device settings.

### 1.3 Data from Others

- Profile mentions and tags by other users
- Comments on your posts/work
- Connection requests from other users
- Job applications you submit are visible to recruiters

## 3. How We Share Information

### 3.1 On Our Platform

**Your Profile**
Your profile is visible to all users by default. You can adjust visibility settings to restrict who sees your profile, contact info, and posts.

**Your Content**
- Posts are visible to your selected audience (everyone/connections/specific communities)
- Work/portfolio items are visible to help you find opportunities
- Comments and reactions are visible to others
- Community membership is visible to other members

**Job Applications**
When you apply for a job, your profile and application are shared with the posting company/recruiter.

**Messages**
Messages you send are visible only to recipients. Group chat messages are visible to all group members.

### 3.2 With Service Providers

We share data with trusted service providers who help us:
- Cloud hosting and infrastructure
- Analytics and measurement
- Payment processing (for paid features)
- Customer support

These providers have access only to data needed to perform their tasks and cannot use it for other purposes.

### 3.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your data when:
- Required by law (subpoena, court order)
- Necessary to protect safety or prevent illegal activity
- Necessary to enforce our agreements
- Necessary to protect our rights or property

### 3.4 Business Transfers

If we merge, sell, or transfer part of our business, your data may be transferred to the new owner, but only as described in this policy.

## 4. Your Choices

### 4.1 Managing Your Data

**Edit Your Profile**
You can update your profile information, work experience, education, skills, and interests at any time through your account settings.

**Control Visibility**
You can adjust who sees your profile, contact info, and content through Privacy settings.

**Delete Content**
You can delete your posts, work items, and other content you have created.

### 4.2 Notifications

You can control which notifications you receive through your account settings:
- Turn off notifications for specific activity types
- Choose email vs push notifications

### 4.3 Account Deletion

You can delete your account at any time. Upon deletion:
- Your profile becomes invisible immediately
- Your data is permanently deleted within 30 days
- Messages you sent in group chats remain visible to other members

## 5. Data Security

We implement security measures including:
- HTTPS encryption for data in transit
- Encrypted password storage
- Regular security monitoring

While we work to protect your data, no system is 100% secure. You also play a role in keeping your account safe by using a strong password.

## 6. Age Requirements

Our Services are only for users aged 16 and older. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 16. If we learn we have collected data from a minor, we will delete it.
