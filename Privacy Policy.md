## 1. Data We Collect

### 1.1 Data You Provide To Us
**Registration and Social Login**
To create an account, you provide data including your name, email address, and a password. You may also choose to register using social logins (e.g., Google, Apple), in which case we receive your profile information (name, email, avatar) from those providers. If you use Apple's **"Hide My Email"** feature, we will collect and use the relay email address generated by Apple to identify your account.

**Profile Information**
You create your Hatch profile based on your selected role(s). Depending on whether you join as a Student, Founder, Working Professional, or Freelancer, you may provide:
* **Education & Experience:** School, degree, graduation year, job title, company name, and employment history.
* **Startup Details:** Startup name, industry, team size, funding stage, and website.
* **Freelance Details:** Primary skills, rates, portfolio URLs, and availability status.
* **Goals & Preferences:** Career goals, project preferences (e.g., short-term vs. long-term), and job preferences (e.g., location, minimum salary, remote work culture).

**Role-Specific Data:**
During onboarding, you provide specific data based on your role. This may include educational details, expected graduation year, startup funding stage, team size, freelancer hourly rates, current availability, career goals, and project preferences.

**Proof of Work & Portfolios**
You may upload portfolios and projects to showcase your skills. This includes project titles, descriptions, dates completed, URLs, tags, and cover images.

**Posting, Communities, and Events**
We collect personal data from you when you provide, post, or upload it to our Services. This includes:
* Creating or joining Communities and RSVPing to Events.
* Posting content (text, media, documents, polls) to the main feed or specific Communities.
* Applying for jobs posted on Hatch.

**Messages**
We collect information about you when you send, receive, or engage with messages in direct or community chats. This includes text, images, audio files, documents, and location shares. Specifically, we collect the text, documents, images, **audio recordings (up to 10 minutes)**, and **location data** that you choose to share in these chats.

### 1.2 Data From Others
**Content and Mentions**
You and others may post content that includes information about you (e.g., tagging you in a post, mentioning you in a Community discussion, or commenting on your Proof of Work).

**Customers and Partners**
We may receive personal data about you when you use the services of our customers and partners, such as a startup or company posting a job and reviewing your application on our platform.

### 1.3 Service Use and Logging
We log usage data when you visit or otherwise use our Services. We use log-ins, cookies, device information, and internet protocol ("IP") addresses to identify you and log your use. We track your interactions, such as the posts you like, the profiles you view, the communities you join, and the searches you perform.

### 1.4 Cookies and Similar Technologies
We use cookies and similar technologies (e.g., pixels and local storage) to collect data to recognize you and your device(s) on, off, and across different Services. You can opt out of certain tracking through your browser or device settings.

## 2. How We Use Your Data

We use your data to provide, support, personalize, and develop our Services. How we use your personal data will depend on which Services you use, how you use them, and the choices you make in your settings.

### 2.1 Services
**Stay Connected & Informed**
Our Services allow you to stay in touch with other builders, classmates, and professionals. We use data about you (such as your profile, your Proof of Work, and your communities) to help others find your profile, suggest connections, and personalize your feed.

**Messaging and Status Indicators:**
To facilitate real-time communication, we process data to show your **"Online" status** and **"Last Seen" time**. We also process **typing indicators** and **read receipts** (showing when a message is delivered and read). You can manage the visibility of your online status and read receipts in your Account/Privacy settings.

**Career and Freelance Opportunities**
We use your data (such as your freelance rates, skills, and job preferences) to recommend jobs, freelance gigs, and startup roles to you. If you apply for a job, your profile and application details are shared with the hiring manager.

**Communities & Events**
We use your data to recommend relevant Communities and Events. If you host an Event or create a Community, we use your profile information to identify you as the admin, creator, or guest speaker.

### 2.2 Communications
We will contact you through email, push notifications, and in-app messages. We send you messages about the availability of our Services, security, and updates. We also send notifications regarding new followers, community invites, event reminders, job alerts, and messages from other Members. You can manage your notification preferences at any time in your account settings.

### 2.3 Security and Investigations
We use your data (including your communications) for security purposes or to prevent or investigate possible fraud or other violations of our User Agreement and Community Guidelines.

## 3. How We Share Information

### 3.1 On Our Services
**Profile and Proof of Work**
Your profile is fully visible to all Members of our Services by default, but you can adjust your visibility settings (e.g., restricting visibility to "Connections" only). Your "Proof of Work" portfolios and the tags associated with them are designed to be visible to help you find opportunities.

**Posts, Likes, Comments, and Communities**
When you share a post, it can be viewed by your selected audience (e.g., "Everyone" or a specific "Community"). When you join a Community or RSVP to an Event, your membership and attendance may be visible to other Members. Social actions (likes, comments, shares) are also visible to others.

**Job Applications**
If you apply for a job or freelance project through Hatch, we will share your profile and application data with the Member or Company that posted the opportunity.

### 3.2 Service Providers
We use others to help us provide our Services (e.g., cloud hosting, maintenance, analysis, audit, payments, fraud detection, and communication delivery). They will have access to your information as reasonably necessary to perform these tasks on our behalf and are obligated not to disclose or use it for other purposes.

## 4. Your Choices & Obligations

### 4.1 Data Retention
We generally retain your personal data as long as you keep your account open or as needed to provide you Services. Even if you only use our Services to update your portfolio every few months, we will retain your information and keep your profile open until you decide to close your account.

### 4.2 Rights to Access and Control Your Personal Data
We provide many choices about the collection, use, and sharing of your data:
* **Delete Data:** You can ask us to erase or delete all or some of your personal data.
* **Change or Correct Data:** You can edit your profile data, adjust your job preferences, and manage your portfolios through your account.
* **Object to, or Limit or Restrict, Use of Data:** You can ask us to stop using all or some of your personal data or limit our use of it via your Privacy Settings.
* **Right to Access and/or Take Your Data:** You can ask us for a copy of your personal data.

### 4.3 Account Closure
If you choose to close your Hatch account, your personal data will stop being visible to others immediately. We will permanently delete your account data within **30 days** of your request. Please note that messages you sent in Group Chats, Community Chats, or Direct Messages may remain visible to the recipients even after your account is deleted.
